Department of Software Engineering

Welcome to our software engineering department at Islamia University of Bahawalpur, where we prepare students to navigate the ever-changing world of software development. With our BS and MS programs, cutting-edge curriculum, and world-class faculty, we equipe our students with the technical skills and principles necessary to succeed. From programming fundamentals to software design and databases, we've got you covered. Our department provides students with the resources they need to pursue their passions and achieve their career goals, whether that's developing software for the latest technology or researching innovative solutions to complex problems. So come join us and discover what it means to be a software engineer!

Our Workshops

Connected Pakistan Workshop

The Connected Pakistan Conference is a major annual event that aims to bring together leading experts, policymakers, and industry stakeholders to discuss the latest trends and innovations in the technology industry in Pakistan. The conference covers a wide range of topics including emerging technologies, entrepreneurship, and digital transformation.With a focus on driving growth and innovation, the Connected Pakistan Conference provides a unique opportunity for participants to learn from industry leaders, network with peers, and gain valuable insights into the latest trends and strategies shaping the technology industry in Pakistan. Whether you're an entrepreneur, investor, or just interested in the latest developments in technology, the Connected Pakistan Conference is a must-attend event that will keep you at the forefront of this rapidly-evolving industry.

Date: 10th June 2022

Time: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM

The Tech Boost Workshop

The Tech Boost workshop is a one-day event focused on providing participants with practical knowledge and insights to help them stay up-to-date with the latest trends and advancements in the technology industry. This workshop is designed to help attendees develop their technical skills, learn about new technologies, and explore emerging trends and topics in the industry. Participants will have the opportunity to attend sessions presented by experts in the field, and they will be able to engage in hands-on activities and exercises to help them apply what they've learned. The Tech Boost workshop is an excellent opportunity for professionals in the technology industry to expand their knowledge and build new connections with like-minded individuals.

Date: 11th June 2023

Time: 9:00 AM - 5:00 PM
